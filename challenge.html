The challenge

Screen reader users also rarely use a mouse. So keyboard use only please. If you can find the answers without seeing the screen, all the better, but to practice you might just have use your eyes to figure out what is happening.

Basically, if you can get VoiceOver to read the answer to you, you can call that task 'done'.
