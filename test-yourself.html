Test yourself more

Using a screen reader is much easier when you can see.

So if you find yourself cheating, then try these things to make it all harder.

  1. Keep the VoiceOver help on the website open while you browse the site (it will stay open while you browse and you can browse with the site in the background
  2. Turn your screen brightness down to 0% or 5% to make it hard to see what you're doing
  3. Place a piece of A4 over your screen to minimise your cheating
  4. Fashion a blindfold from anything you can find
  5. Change VoiceOver settings to use a faster Voice. Blind users will often use a very very fast voice!
