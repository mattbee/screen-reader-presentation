Screenreader Users

Screen readers are hard to use. As with all technology, the more time you invest in them, the better you get at using them.

If you're visually impaired, you tend not to have much of a choice if you want to use a computer, so they often become power users.

Power users are so efficient and knowledgable about how to use a screenreader, they use them in very different ways to a sighted person, like Matt here, Hey.
